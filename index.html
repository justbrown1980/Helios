We need to change for the future

Rather than farm more hectares of land,let's mitigate senseless post-harvest losses which account for up to 60% in some areas in the world especially SubSaharan African.

By changing farming practices especially in the post-harvest period when the fruits and vegetables leave the farmgate, immense cost savings will be made by providing fresh and nutritious food for the rapidly growing local population and an invaluable source of revenue when these produce are exported to Europe especially in winter when these products are in high demand.

By the numbers..

1.3 Billion Tonnes

The amount of food wasted each year,equals one third of the food produced in the world each year.

200 million

The number of people that could be fed by the amount of food currently wasted in Europe alone

4.4 gigatonnes

The amount of greenhouse gas emissions (GHG) per year caused by food wastage.
